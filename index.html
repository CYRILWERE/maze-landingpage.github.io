Developed as part of my portfolio at Holberton School, this project showcases my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Instructions

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move through the maze:

  • Up Arrow: Move up
  • Down Arrow: Move down
  • Left Arrow: Move left
  • Right Arrow: Move right
